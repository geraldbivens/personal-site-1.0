These projects differ dramatically in the intention behind their creation—some of them are apps that I built while attending a coding bootcamp, some of them are the equivalent of notes that I've made as I continue to learn new languages and new technologies (while also deepening my understanding of what I've aleady learned), and the rest are best characterized as not so serious ongoing endeavors that represent either things that I'm interested in, and want to have before me, or stray nonsensical noise that comes into my head that I thought I might get out by putting them here.

Confucius said, 'To know that we know what we know, and that we do not know what we do not know, that is true knowledge.' When one man has reduced a fact of the imagination to be a fact to his understanding, I foresee that all men will at length establish their lives on that basis."1
